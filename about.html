About Me

Hello, my name is Bailey Kelly, the creator of SprueLab. I started building Gunpla in May of 2023, when a family friend introduced the hobby to me. I love working with my hands, so I knew this would be a hobby that I would enjoy.

My Gunpla Journey

The first kit I bought was the Gundam Helios from Hobby Lobby. The things that made me buy this kit in particular was the simple color scheme, and the back pack. The back piece has wings, along with waist mounted guns, which the other kits did not. After building that kit, I got a couple of kits as a graduation gift, along with much better tools that made the building process more smooth, and made the kit look much better after it was built. After I had built a few kits, I started experimenting with panel lining, and water slide decals for a little extra detail to help them pop.
